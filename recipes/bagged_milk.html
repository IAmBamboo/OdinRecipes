Bagged Milk for Cats!


Fresh and tasty bagged milk for your cat

For centuries, mankind has strived to perfect the technology of bagging milk for the sole purpose of pleasing our cat overlords.

Now in the 21st century, we have perfected our skills of producing bagged milk. This will ensure humankind's survival under the rule of the cats.

Ingredients & Tools required

  • Bagged Milk (It must be bagged!)
  • Scissors
  • Bowl

The process!

  1. Cut the bagged milk by one of the corners using scissors.
  2. Gently pour the milk into the bowl
  3. Serve to the cat in a professional manner
