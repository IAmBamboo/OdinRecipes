Bagged Milk for Cats!


Ingredients & Tools required

  • Bagged Milk (It must be bagged!)
  • Scissors
  • Bowl

The process!

  1. Cut the bagged milk by one of the corners using scissors.
  2. Gently pour the milk into the bowl
  3. Serve to the cat in a professional manner
