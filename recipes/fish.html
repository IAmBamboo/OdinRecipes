Fresh and tasty bagged milk for your cat

Grilled Fish is a popular recipe invented by cat lover Keanu Reeves. Due to his love for cats, he sought out to invent a new meaty dish for them.

Thanks to the popularity of TikTok exploding, Keanu Reeve's Grilled Fish recipe spread among TikTok users like wildfire. It is a common breakfast meal served to high ranking cat officials now.

Ingredients & Tools required

  • Raw fish
  • A grill or an oven
  • A Plate

The process!

  1. Place the raw fish onto a grill or inside an oven
  2. Grill/Cook the fish on each side until your cat gives you a sign of approval
  3. Serve to the cat in a professional manner and avoid being jealous
