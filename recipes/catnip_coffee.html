Catnip Coffee!

Catnip Coffee! Yum!


Because your cats don't have enough ZOOMIES

Catnip Coffee was first made by the ancient Greek to physically enhance the abilities of their cat overlords before a battle.

However in modern times, it is used to sharpen the senses of cat e-sport pros. Many organizers are demanding a ban on the drink.

Ingredients & Tools required

  • Fresh catnip
  • Hot water
  • Coffee beans of your choice
  • Blender
  • Your cat's favorite mug!

The process!

  1. Place your coffee beans and catnip into a blender
  2. Blend for 10 seconds or more
  3. Place the powder into your favorite mug
  4. Pour hot water into your mug, stir the hot water and powder together
  5. Serve to the cat in a professional and clean manner
